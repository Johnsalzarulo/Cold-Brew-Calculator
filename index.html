Step 1. Grind and Weigh Your Coffee

  • Put your pitcher or jar on top of your scale.
  • Hit "Tare" so the scale measures zero.
  • Add your Coffee until you hit your target weight.
  • NOTE: If you are using "cheesecloth" to filter, you'll want to put that inside your jar or pitcher when you "tare" the scale, then add your coffee into the cheesecloth inside the pitcher.

Step 2. Add Brew Water

  • Now that you have coffee in the jar,
  • Hit "Tare" so the scale measures zero again.
  • Add only xx Grams of your cold or room temp filtered water.
  • Cover your pitcher or jar and leave it in a cool dark place.

Step 3. Wait 12 - 18 Hours

  • I usually do 12, 18 does make it a little richer.
  • If you are able it's good to stir or shake the jar a couple times while it brews, although not essential.

Step 4. Filter

  • Grab your chemex or other filter method and pour out the coffee so it filters.
  • If you did it with the cheesecloth simply pull out the cheesecloth with all the grounds inside.

Step 5. Dilute

  • Put your jar or pitcher of filtered brewed coffee concentrate on your scale and "tare" it again.
  • Add the last xx Grams of your cold or room temp filtered water.
